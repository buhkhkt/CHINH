Climatic Features:

 Trà Vinh province is situated in a tropical monsoon climate zone, characterized by hot and humid weather. The climate is divided into two distinct seasons:

the rainy season,

the dry season.

The average annual temperature in Trà Vinh province ranges from 25 to 27 degrees Celsius. The province experiences low and unpredictable rainfall, with an average annual humidity level fluctuating between 83 and 85%. The driest months are typically February and March. Trà Vinh is affected by the fringes of tropical storms or tropical low-pressure systems, which significantly impact

the production

and

lives

of the coastal population.
