Types of Resources:

 Trà Vinh province has approximately 9,539 hectares of forested area. The forest coverage rate is 4.1%. The province is classified into 5 land groups and 18 types of soil. Among these, saline land is the largest in terms of area. The main types of minerals include mineral water and common

construction materials

: riverbed sand, coastal sand, alluvial sand, clay,... In addition, Trà Vinh possesses an extensive fishing ground with many natural fisheries, including various types of fish, shrimp, and squid. The province has significant aquatic resources that have great exploitation potential, featuring many high-value economic seafood species.
